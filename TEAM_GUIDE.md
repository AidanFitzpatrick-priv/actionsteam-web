# How to sign up & get access

## Sign up with an invite

1. Ask a **Sub Lead, Lead, Aux, Adm, or Management** member for an invite link.
2. Open the link (looks like `https://…/signup?invite=…`).
3. Enter email, username, and password (minimum 10 characters).
4. After sign-up you are logged in and can open **Schedule** and **Tracker** for the active month.

Do not share invite links in public channels. Each link is usually single-use and expires after 7 days.

## Who to ask for access

| Need | Ask |
|------|-----|
| New team member account | Sub Lead, Lead, Aux, Adm, or Management |
| Promote to Sub Lead | Aux, Adm, or Management |
| New month / active month | Aux, Adm, or Management |
| Staff list / action types / gangs | Aux, Adm, or Management |
| Account disabled / password issues | Aux, Adm, or Management |

## Everyday use

- **Home** — links to the active month’s schedule and tracker.
- **Stats** — Action Stats (win rates, gang attendance, etc.).
- **Goals** — your weekly action/booking points (Mon–Sun).
- **Invites** — visible if you can create invites (Sub Lead+).
